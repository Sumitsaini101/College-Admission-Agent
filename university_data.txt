New Era Engineering Colledge Admission Guidelines 2026:
The B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering program requires a minimum of 60% aggregate in 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
The tuition fee for the B.Tech CSE program is ₹1,20,000 per semester.

The B.Tech CSE curriculum includes core subjects like Database Management Systems (DBMS), Operating Systems (OS), and Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA).

Students have access to advanced networking labs equipped with Cisco Packet Tracer for hands-on routing configuration.

The campus features an AI-Powered Library Management System built on Agile Scrum methodologies.
